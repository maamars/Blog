PABEBE GURLS SLAYING

Personal Reflection

Participating in the Research Festival 2025 was one of the most memorable and rewarding experiences of my academic journey. As a participant, I felt both nervous and excited because our group, DevTech, had the opportunity to present our system entitled Food Reservation and Real-Time Availability Monitoring System for Saint Columban College Canteen. This event allowed us to showcase the hard work, dedication, and knowledge that we invested in developing our project.

During our presentation, I realized that research is more than just creating a system or completing a requirement. It is about finding practical solutions to real problems. Our project was designed to help improve the canteen services of Saint Columban College by making food reservations easier and providing real-time updates on food availability. Knowing that our study could contribute to a more efficient and convenient canteen experience made me feel proud and fulfilled.

The festival also taught me the importance of teamwork and cooperation. As members of DevTech, we shared responsibilities, supported one another, and worked together despite challenges and pressure. Through this experience, I learned that success is achieved when every team member contributes their best effort and remains committed to a common goal.

One of the happiest moments of the event was when our group received the award as the Top Best Performing Team. Receiving this recognition was truly unexpected and overwhelming. It made all our sacrificies, sleepless nights, and hard work worth it. This achievement motivated me to believe more in my abilities and inspired me to continue striving for excellence.

Overall, the Research Festival 2025 was a valuable experience that helped me grow academically and personally. It strengthened my confidence, communication skills, and appreciation for research and innovation. I am grateful to be part of DevTech and proud of what we accomplished together. This experience will always remind me that dedication, teamwork, and perseverance lead to success.
