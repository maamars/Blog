My Research Festival 2025

November 7, 2025

On November 7, 2025, we proudly participated in the Research Festival, an event where students showcase their Web Systems and Capstone Projects. This festival was the culmination of months of hard work, teamwork, and creativity. It gave us the chance to present our systems to instructors, peers, and guests, and explain the real-world problems our projects aim to solve.
