Acknowledgement

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to everyone who has been part of my On-the-Job Training (OJT) journey. This experience has been a meaningful step in my growth, both professionally and personally.

First and foremost, I am deeply thankful to my supervisor and also to our dean for their guidance, patience, and support. Your willingness to share your knowledge and expertise has helped me gain valuable skills and confidence in my chosen field.

This OJT experience has been a significant milestone in my life, and I will carry the lessons and memories with me as I move forward in my career.

Thank you very much.
