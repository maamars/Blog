About NCIP

The National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP) is the primary government agency that formulates and implements policies, plans, and programs for the recognition, promotion, and protection of the rights and well-being of Indigenous Cultural Communities/Indigenous Peoples (ICCs/IPs).

I am currently serving as an intern in the Technical Management Service Division (TMSD), specifically under the supervision of the Planning Officer.

Mission & Vision

Vision

"An agency that is responsive to the aspirations of Indigenous Peoples and committed to the protection and promotion of their rights and well-being."

Mission

"To formulate and implement policies, plans and programs to promote and protect the rights and well-being of IPs and the recognition of their ancestral domains as well as their rights thereto."

Internship Placement

Technical Management Service Division (TMSD)

Responsible for the management and planning of various technical programs aimed at supporting indigenous communities.
